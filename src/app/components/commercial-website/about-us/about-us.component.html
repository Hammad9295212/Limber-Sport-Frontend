OUR STORY

How it all Began

After all the turmoil of being separated from the ones we love or at least engaging in some simple social activities, we decided the world needed a better solution.
We began Limber as a way to reconnect with our local friends, family, and community through the shared love of sports. It doesnâ€™t matter if you love chess or rugby. Being able to engage in a team sport where you get to speak with another human being face to face again instead of behind a screen is the best way to improve your health and mental abilities.
Limber is a simple solution to empower clubs, individuals, markets, facilities, and coaches to get back into the swing of community involvement once again using an easy-to-understand platform that makes communication and management a breeze.

MISSION

Our Fundamental Ideal

Limber is designed to help people get healthy and enjoy social activities through the pastime of sports. We provide a centralized platform to help coaches, teams, clubs, organizations, facilities, and individuals improve and create opportunities for everyone to get back into an active lifestyle.
